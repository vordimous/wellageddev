playbook: |
  Ambient ~91°F, drum hot from two prior roasts. **Maracaturra is low-density** — it'll move fast through development. Watch FC closely; don't miss the window.

  1. Confirm drum cooled ~7 min. Empty chaff tray.
  2. Weigh 227 g green. Load drum, **P5 / ½ lb**, Start.
  3. Chopstick in door. Phone timer running.
  4. **Listen for FC** — likely 9–11 min today; could be quieter than Brazil/Guatemala (Maracaturra tends to crack soft). Mark time-to-FC.
  5. **Drop trigger:** 30–60s *after* FC ends, before any second crack. Hit **C**.
  6. Cool cycle ~10 min.
  7. Weigh roasted.
  8. **Between this and Ethiopia: cool drum 10–12 min, door wide open.** Drum will be hottest of the day; the Ethiopia is the most delicate.
