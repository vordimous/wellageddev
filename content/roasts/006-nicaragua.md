playbook: |
  Built from the Behmor 2000 AB Plus manual V30. Central American hard bean → **Auto Mode P1** (manual default for Centrals). Pro sample-roasting technique to finish.

  **Plan rationale:**
  - 227 g charge on ½ lb weight setting → 12:00 program time, 75% shutoff fires at **9:00 elapsed** (need to press START or C).
  - Target: City+ (Light-medium). 227 g is well under the 336 g manual ceiling for darker control, so we have headroom if FC runs late.
  - Maracaturra's large screen size means **internal development lags surface color** (per Sweet Maria's) — wait through the FC tail rather than dropping at first snap.

  **Sequence:**
  1. Empty chaff tray. Weigh 227 g green into drum, close drum clasp.
  2. **Preheat (first roast of session):** drum OUT, press ½ lb + any profile + START, let run **1:30**, press **OFF**. Do not exceed 1:30 — longer locks out the roast.
  3. Insert loaded drum (drum is asymmetric — it only fits one way). Insert chaff tray. Close door.
  4. Press **½ lb** + **P1** + **START**. Start phone timer in parallel.
  5. Listen for FC from ~7 min on. (Behmor's insulated door muffles FC — community technique is to wedge a chopstick in the door slot for better audio; **not in the manual**, use if needed.)
  6. **75% shutoff at 9:00 elapsed:** display blinks `un:30`. Press **START** to continue, OR press **C** (also bypasses Err 7). Stay near the machine.
  7. **Listen for FC.** Cracks may be soft — mark time-to-FC the instant of the first audible pop.
  8. **Pro sample-roasting technique** (manual Part 5, page 12-13): **10–15 seconds after FC starts**, press **P3** (drops to 50% power) then press **D** (raises drum speed). Lets exothermic momentum carry development without scorching.
  9. May need to add time using **+** or **C** to extend through development.
  10. **Drop trigger (Light-medium / City+):** **30–60s into development**, before FC fully winds down. Press **Cool**. If the FC tail is still rolling at 60s+ because of the Maracaturra development lag, holding another 30s into Full City territory is within SM's range — just don't chase past that.
  11. Cool cycle ~10 min. After beans stop snapping (~1:30 into cool), open door for faster cool airflow.
  12. Weigh roasted. Target loss ~13–15%, DTR 17–20%.
  13. **Allow 1 HOUR between this and any next roast** (manual Part 4 Important Reminder #4).

  **What to watch:**
  - Soft, dispersed FC pops are possible (003 observation, not a documented characteristic). If unsure where FC starts, my judgment call: treat the *first sustained* pop as t=0 for the P3+D press — the manual doesn't address soft-cracking varieties.
  - Pale color at FC + bland smell = under-developed (this was 003's failure mode for Maracaturra). Hold through the FC tail before dropping.
  - Heavy bluish smoke or any 2nd-crack snap = imminent fire risk. Press Cool immediately.
  - Never roast past 10 seconds into 2C.
