playbook: |
  Ambient ~91°F, drum has been running for hours. **This is the delicate one** — protect the florals. The whole game is "drop early."

  1. Confirm drum cooled **10–12 min**, door wide open. Empty chaff tray thoroughly (lots of accumulated chaff by now).
  2. Weigh 227 g green. Load drum, **P5 / ½ lb**, Start.
  3. Chopstick in door immediately — FC will come fast.
  4. **Listen for FC** — could be as early as 8–10 min today. Mark time-to-FC the *instant* you hear the first pop.
  5. **Drop trigger:** 15–45s after FC starts. **Before** any oil sheen, before FC even fully rolls. Hit **C** the moment you hit your window — err on the early side.
  6. Cool cycle ~10 min.
  7. Weigh roasted. Expect ~12–14% loss; if you see >15% you went too far.
  8. Rest 24h — Ethiopians especially benefit from full rest before tasting.
